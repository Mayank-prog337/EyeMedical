Vision Care Excellence

Aurobindo Netralaya provides world-class eye care services with state-of-the-art technology and experienced specialists. Our commitment is to deliver the highest quality eye care with compassion and innovation.

Our Specialized Departments & Treatments

Comprehensive eye care services for all vision needs — surgical, medical and rehabilitation.

Cataract Surgery

Advanced phacoemulsification with premium intraocular lenses including toric and multifocal options.

Retina & Vitreous

Management of diabetic retinopathy, macular diseases, retinal detachments and intravitreal therapies.

Cornea & Anterior Segment

Corneal transplants, DSEK/DMEK, keratoconus care and corneal cross-linking for stabilizing ectatic disease.

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Child-friendly evaluations, squint surgery, amblyopia therapy and pediatric retina care.

Glaucoma Clinic

Early detection with OCT/visual fields and medical, laser or surgical glaucoma management.

Ocular Oncology & Neuro-Ophthalmology

Diagnosis and multidisciplinary care for ocular tumors and vision-related neurological conditions.

Advanced Eye Care Technologies

We utilize the latest diagnostics and treatment platforms to provide precise and personalized care.

Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)

High-resolution cross-sectional retinal imaging for macular and optic nerve assessment.

Wavefront & Topography

Customized corneal and refractive mapping for premium vision correction plans.

Femtosecond & Excimer Lasers

Precision refractive surgeries (LASIK/SMILE) and laser-assisted steps in cataract care.

Phaco & Micro-Incision Surgery

Modern cataract platforms for rapid recovery and safer outcomes.

Diagnostic Fundus Cameras

Widefield and digital fundus imaging for comprehensive retinal documentation.

Low-Vision & Vision Rehabilitation

Adaptive devices and therapy to maximize remaining vision for daily living.
